Using R and Mesquite together  

Wayne Maddison & Hilmar Lapp

Mesquite is a graphical application for phylogenetic and evolutionary analysis; R is a system for statistical computation primarily oriented around text-based programming. Two packages provide for communication between the two:

  • Mesquite.R enables Mesquite to make use of functions in R.
  • RMesquite enables R programs to make use of Mesquite, either as a graphical interface for editing trees and data, or to provide its broad array of phylogenetic calculations to R.
